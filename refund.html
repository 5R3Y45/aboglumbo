Refund and Exchange Policy

At Abo Glumbo, we strive to provide top-notch services through our expert workers. However, we understand that sometimes things may not go as expected. Our refund and exchange policy is designed to ensure customer satisfaction.

Refund Policy

  • If you're unsatisfied with the work performed by our experts, we offer a refund within 14 days of service completion.
  • Refunds will be processed within 2 weeks of receiving your refund request.
  • To initiate a refund, please contact our customer support team via our phone number: 00966530439333.

Responsibility and Liability

  • Abo Glumbo takes full responsibility for any mistakes or damages caused by our workers.
  • In case of any errors or damages, we will work promptly to rectify the issue or provide a full refund.

Eligibility for Refund

  • Refunds are only applicable for services booked through the Abo Glumbo platform.
  • Refunds will not be processed for services partially completed or cancelled by the customer.

Exchange Policy

  • If you're not satisfied with the initial worker assigned, we'll provide an alternative expert at no additional cost.
  • To request an exchange, please contact our customer support team within 24 hours of service commencement.

Cancellation Policy

  • Customers can cancel services up to 24 hours before the scheduled appointment without incurring any charges.
  • Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee, depending on the service provider's policies.

Dispute Resolution

  • In case of disputes, Abo Glumbo's customer support team will work to resolve issues amicably.
  • Our decision on refunds and exchanges will be final.
